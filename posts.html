Engineer’s Day 2025: Inspiring Dreams, Guiding Futures

“Engineering is the art of shaping ideas into reality – and guiding young minds is the first blueprint of that creation.” On Engineer’s Day 2025, Engineering India Club, Ramdeobaba University, organized a career guidance session at Nagpur Madhyamik Vidyalaya, Katol Road, Nagpur, for students of Classes 9th and 10th. The session focused on helping students understand streams after Class 10, diverse career paths beyond engineering, and the importance of aligning passion with future goals.

Through an interactive career talk, exploration of Science, Commerce, and Arts, a specially designed flowchart of opportunities, and the “Dream Behind the Flowchart” activity, students were encouraged to write down and own their dream professions. An open Q&A on exams, scholarships, and colleges helped clear doubts and build confidence. The day truly lived the motto of the event: “Inspiring Dreams, Guiding Futures.”
