Code of Conduct

Our commitment to creating a respectful and inclusive community

1. Our Commitment

Engineering India RBU is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members, regardless of background, identity, or experience level. We expect all participants to follow this code of conduct in all our activities, events, and communications.

2. Expected Behavior

We encourage the following behaviors:

  • Respect: Treat everyone with dignity and respect, regardless of their background, identity, or opinions
  • Inclusion: Welcome newcomers and help them feel part of our community
  • Collaboration: Work together constructively and support each other's growth
  • Professionalism: Maintain professional conduct in all interactions
  • Open-mindedness: Be open to different perspectives and ideas
  • Accountability: Take responsibility for your actions and their impact on others

3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

  • Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
  • Offensive comments related to gender, race, religion, nationality, sexual orientation, or disability
  • Inappropriate physical contact or unwelcome sexual attention
  • Disruptive behavior that interferes with events or activities
  • Sharing inappropriate content or spam
  • Violation of privacy or confidentiality
  • Any behavior that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome

4. Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code of conduct, please report it immediately:

  • Contact any member of the leadership team
  • Email us at: engineeringindiarbu@gmail.com
  • Speak with event organizers or moderators

All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly.

5. Consequences

Violations of this code of conduct may result in:

  • Warning and education about appropriate behavior
  • Temporary suspension from events or activities
  • Permanent removal from the organization
  • Reporting to appropriate authorities if necessary

6. Event-Specific Guidelines

6.1 Online Events

  • Use appropriate language and maintain professional communication
  • Respect others' time and avoid unnecessary interruptions
  • Follow platform-specific guidelines and etiquette

6.2 In-Person Events

  • Arrive on time and follow event schedules
  • Respect the venue and other participants
  • Follow health and safety guidelines

7. Social Media and Online Presence

When representing Engineering India RBU online:

  • Maintain the same standards of respect and professionalism
  • Do not post content that could harm our organization's reputation
  • Respect others' privacy and obtain consent before sharing photos or information
  • Use our official channels appropriately

8. Academic Integrity

We expect all members to:

  • Maintain high standards of academic integrity
  • Give proper credit for others' work and ideas
  • Not engage in plagiarism or cheating
  • Support each other's academic growth ethically

9. Community Service Standards

When participating in community service activities:

  • Show respect and empathy for those we serve
  • Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
  • Follow all safety protocols and guidelines
  • Represent our organization positively in the community

10. Continuous Improvement

This code of conduct is a living document that we regularly review and update. We welcome feedback and suggestions for improvement from our community members.

11. Questions and Clarifications

If you have questions about this code of conduct or need clarification on any point, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Email: engineeringindiarbu@gmail.com
  • Phone: +91 7821903003
  • Address: RBU Campus, Nagpur, Maharashtra, India - 440010

12. Acknowledgment

By participating in Engineering India RBU activities, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to follow this code of conduct. We believe that by working together with respect and integrity, we can create a truly inclusive and supportive community.

Remember: Our goal is to create a community where everyone can thrive, learn, and contribute to making a positive impact in society. Let's work together to maintain the highest standards of conduct and create an environment we can all be proud of.
