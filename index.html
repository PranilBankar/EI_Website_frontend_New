About Engineering India RBU

More than just a club, we are a thriving community that brings together engineering minds to create meaningful change in society while fostering personal and professional growth.

Our Community

Engineering India RBU serves as a bridge connecting current engineering students, distinguished alumni, and experienced professionals from across Nagpur's engineering landscape. Through our diverse initiatives, we've created a platform where theoretical knowledge meets practical application, where individual talents combine to address real-world challenges.

Our Core Values

Our Mission

Social Impact
